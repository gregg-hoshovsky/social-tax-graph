The Social Tax Graph

The Social Tax graph is a crowd sourcing solution that aims to remove the congressional deadlock and giving you more of a voice. Currently we give our money to congress in a big wad and they give it to their favorite charity, themselves, their cronies and their lobbies. When told to control government spending they simply take the same money for us and lower the funding for the defense and welfare of the nation. So whether you are a Libertarian, Republican, Tea partier, Liberal or a Progressive, we can join together and determine how to spend that money. Use the Enter your opinion link to start. Simply add your opinion anonymously or login to be a full participant in the social tax graph community.

3. Did you know?

Make your opinion known
Using the simple interface add the % you want to support each category.
Add new categories
Use the menus and navigate to the page to add your own categories. this will require you to login w/ your facebook account.
Create complex hierarchies( in tree format )
Using a tree format you can give more guidance to congressional spending. You can even "defund" parts of a category. Say you want to fund defense spending but not subisidize the CEO pay.
Compare your choices to those of your friends and the community at large
See the comparative graphs to see how your choices rate to others. Look at the social tax graph to see how different people organize their tax world view.
Get recommenders to other categories to fund ( or Defund)
To create a link you just have to click its target (use Views / Show targets to display targets).
